Who I am

Designer & developer based out of Zürich

Hi, welcome to my page :) Although I originally trained as a philosopher, I’m now a self-taught front-end web developer. You might say I’m a front-end-philosopher! I’ve tried to use the self-discipline and logical thinking that studying philosophy taught me to learn how to code.

I started out practising small coding ‘challenges’ in Python, before moving on to an online course, where I also learned some SQLite. When I explored all the different types of software development jobs I found I lean most toward front-end web development. I believe this fits the best with my personality: creative and results-driven. From then on I focussed on learning HTML, CSS, Javascript, Bootstrap and responsive web design. While this gave me a good overview of software development, I’m certainly still eager to learn more.
